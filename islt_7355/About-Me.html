Biography

I teach the architectural structures sequence at Drury University and am a registered architect. I have taught full-time for nearly twenty years and have developed a teaching repertoire exceeding twenty courses for architecture and engineering university degree granting programs. The courses range from architectural design to engineering technologies.

I have over thirty refereed papers and two dozen presentations mostly regarding emergent pedagogies and the nature of digital technologies intervening in higher education. I have presented my work at venues such as the University of Oxford and the Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule. I earned two national AIA awards for innovative curriculum development through the AIA Technology in Architectural Practice (TAP) BIM Awards. I was published twice in The AIA Report on University Research (Volumes 4 and 5). I am most proud of being the editor-in-chief of the Architectural Graphic Standards (Student Edition).

Drury University faculty member since 2009
Professor of Architecture since 2021 (tenured 2015)
