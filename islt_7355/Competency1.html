Competency 1 - Design and Development of Learning Systems

Description
The ISLT 9458 Technology and Assessment class permitted a deep dive into the technology being applied my capstone structures course for assessment. My students are asked to develop a post-and-beam structural framing solution that follows a given schematic design. The performance assessment project contextualized the learning experience, responded to an essential question, followed the GRASPS task design prompts, required an assessment plan and scoring guide, a detailed narrative describing the technology, and incorporates changes from peer reviews. The biggest challenge was the creation of a numerical rubric that correlates to a visualization-based project.

Reflection
The class project had a direct and immediate impact on my teaching. I was able to incorporate a detailed rubric to assess one of the learning objectives. Due to the creative nature of architecture, faculty seem to frown upon rubrics and prefer subjectivity. I find them extremely useful and the students have responded positively as a result of more meaningful learning and assessments.
