Competency 3 - Leadership and Social Responsibility

Description
The ISLT 9471 Instructional Systems Design class taught me how to design instructional sequences based upon the learners’ needs. The course occurred during the pandemic shutdown which informed our project of virtual field trips. In a world where physical distancing is increasingly important to inhibit the community spread of viral infections, an urgent need exists to develop distance classroom learning engagements. Virtual field trips will bring external learning experiences into the classroom through local or international adventures. A training session was developed for the K‐12 educators to develop a personalized virtual field trip for their classrooms using Google Tour Builder. This was a group project. My individual accountabilities are detailed on page 3.

Reflection
The class project influences how I approach the application of technology today. I am more socially sensitive due to how we interact as a result of the pandemic. I have embraced the Moodle LMS. The classes has morphed into hybrids. I still conduct classroom lectures, but the LMS is an equal component where learning also occurs.
